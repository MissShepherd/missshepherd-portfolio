Messy route, solid build.

My route into development was not neat or linear. I have restarted, doubted myself, learned things the hard way, and kept going. That persistence now shapes how I work: I break complicated ideas into clear systems, leave room for personality, and build interfaces that remain readable even when the visual world gets loud.

01

Structure

I use Angular, TypeScript, SCSS, routing, reusable components, and responsive layouts to turn complicated information into interfaces that make sense.

02

Worldbuilding

Games, music, animation, roleplay, and character design influence how I approach visual identity. I want an interface to feel like it belongs to its own world, not like content dropped into a template.

03

Human First

My brain is often loud, busy, and full of interconnected ideas. That makes me care deeply about clear hierarchy, responsive behaviour, and designs that remain understandable without losing their personality.
